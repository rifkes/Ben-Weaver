Ben Weaver

We are a strategic and creative agency, based in London, working with culturally programmed organizations, forward thinking brands, creative businesses and talented people.


Established twenty years ago, our clients include museums, galleries, arts organizations, retailers, consumer brands, media owners, public bodies and creative studios.


We develop brand strategies, visual identities, cultural programs, art projects, special events, creative collaborations, brand partnerships, and communication campaigns.
