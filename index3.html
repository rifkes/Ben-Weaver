About

A London based strategic and creative agency established in 1998. We work with culturally programmed organizations, visionary brands and creative businesses.


Our clients include museums, galleries, retailers, consumer brands, broadcasters, public bodies and creative studios. We develop brand strategies, cultural programs, art projects, curated experiences and communication campaigns.
